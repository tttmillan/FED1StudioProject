Finance For Everyone

Different Groups

We all have to start somewhere.

Beginners

Basic information about how to manage a person's finances. Budgeting, Saving, and Tips to Control Spending. We all have to start somewhere. The easiest way to start is to use only cash. That way a person can see where every dollar goes to. If a person runs out of cash then they can not buy anything.

Moderate Knowledge

This is when a person knows how to budget and spend money moderately but still hit a few bumps on the way and always seems to have things come up. This is more about how to break down spending per week and using the envelope method and to build up envelopes to have a emergency fund and to stay on track.

Expert

All ready doing good they have credit cards and know how to use and manipulate the rewards if desired. How to find good deals when they come up and invest in mutliple things. This is where we all share things that we learned along the way to help others and find new information to keep advancing.
