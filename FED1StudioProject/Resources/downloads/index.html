Starting A Budget

This finance website is for everyone from beginners to more advanced.

Simple Budget

A Simple Budget is writing down how much cash(income) a person brings in each month. This includes making a separate list for monthly bills and totaling the amount of expenses to ensure that the expenses are less than the income.

Income

Income is the cash coming into a household. The top of the list should be guaranteed income and then any other income that varies month to month or week to week.

Expenses

Expenses is all money going out of a household starting with bills that stay the same every month that is a necessity. For example Housing is at the top of the list with Electricity, Water, Gas. From most important to least important.
