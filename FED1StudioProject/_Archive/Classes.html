Different Classes

Each course is 2 hours long. All beginner courses are on Monday and Tuesdays. All intermediate courses are on Wednesday and Thursdays. All advanced courses are on Fridays and Saturdays.

Savings

Types of Credit

Class Topic: Different types of credit and how to choose and when to use each type.

Types of Insurance

Class Topic: Different types of Insurance and which is needed when.

College Costs

Class Topic: How to save and pay for College.
