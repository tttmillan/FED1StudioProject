Starting A Budget

This is a website for everyone from beginners to more advance.

Simple Budget

A Simple Budget is writing down how much cash a person brings in on the left side of a piece of paper in list format. Then write all the bills on the right side of the paper.

Income

Income is the cash coming into a household. The top of the left side of list should be guaranteed income and then any side income that varies month to month or week to week.

Expenses

Expenses is all money going out of a household starting with bills that stay the same every month that is a need not a want. For example Housing to top with Electricity, Water, Gas. From most important to least important.
